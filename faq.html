Where did you get the idea for this?

When some people play games like Jackbox on stream, they do not have a second monitor or a mobile device to use, and others perfer playing on the computer. What was painful about it that I had to keep switching tabs between the stream and the game, and sometimes I end up missing the opportunity to answer something because I was on the stream tab and not the game tab. I did try putting my browser windows side-by-side, but that made the stream so tiny unless I condensed the chat, which I don't want to do because I want to see the chat as well. Popping out the stream and chat can also be tedious. I made this mainly for myself, so I would be able to play the Jackbox games while seeing the stream and chat at the same time, so I wouldn't miss something.

What sites are supported?

Twitch, Smashcast, Mixer, YouTube, Mobcrush, and Vimm TV.

How do I use it?

Simply select the platform the streamer is streaming on, type in their username (or Video ID for YouTube), select the game, and click Go!

I'm a streamer and want to provide an easy way to provide this to my viewers. How do I do that?

You can click the "Get Direct Link" to get a URL that will automatically load your stream, and you can share that URL with your audience.

What games are supported?

All Jackbox games and Use Your Words are currently supported. Pretend You're Xyzzy and Azala are currently in beta support.

Will you support [insert platform here]?

I am open to adding more platforms, but if a site does not provide a way to embed at least the stream, I cannot support it.

Will you support [insert game here]?

I am open to adding more games, but if a site does not allow embedding, I cannot support it. Also, this should go without saying that if the game is only playable in an app, and not on a website, I won't be able to support it.

Will there be a mobile version of this?

I don't see a mobile version of this happening. The main reason is that the stream and game window would be too small to see, especially on phones.

I found a bug, or I have an idea or would like to suggest a game or platform for this. Where can I do that?!

Feel free to submit an issue on GitHub, and I will look into it. Pull requests are also welcome.

How to use the parameters:

site= - What streaming platform to use. Acceptable values are twitch, smashcast, mixer, youtube, mobcrush, vimm. Defaults to Twitch if omitted.

stream= - The username of the streamer, or the Video ID in the case of YouTube. If omitted, shows the help and news sections instead.

game= - What game to load. Acceptable values are jackbox, useyourwords, xyzzy, and azala. Defaults to Jackbox if omitted.

Example

https://decimic.github.io/partygameviewer/?site=mixer&stream=randomuser123&game=useyourwords

That URL will load up randomuser123's stream on Mixer, along with Use Your Words.

Limitations

  • Embeds do not count as organic viewers towards partnership on Twitch.
  • Moderation tools, bits, and whispers are not available in embedded Twitch chat.
  • Extensions are not available through embeds on Twitch.
  • Channel Points cannot be earned through embeds on Twitch.
  • There is no way to disable skill effects or rallies on the embedded Mixer chat.
  • MixPlay is unavailable through embeds on Mixer.
  • If a Mixer stream is in a costream, chats cannot be prevented from being merged. However, the stream can be prevented from showing other streams.
  • Most browser extensions, including MixrElixr, do not work. FFZ and BTTV do appear to work but may have limited functionality.
  • Some YouTube streams cannot be embedded (the streamer can disable this, and some channels may not have embedding available to them).
  • Vimm TV chat cannot be embedded.
