Support has been added for StreamMe!

URL Parameters Added!

You can now directly link for the viewer to go right to that stream! (To make it easier, I've made the Get Direct Link button!)

How to use the parameters:

site= - What streaming platform to use. Acceptable values are twitch, smashcast, mixer, youtube, and streamme. Defaults to Twitch if omitted.

stream= - The username of the streamer, or the Video ID in the case of YouTube. If omitted, shows the help and news sections instead.

game= - What game to load. Acceptable values are jackbox and useyourwords. Defaults to Jackbox if omitted.

Example

https://decimic.github.io/partygameviewer/?site=mixer&stream=randomuser123&game=useyourwords

That URL will load up randomuser123's stream on Mixer, along with Use Your Words.
